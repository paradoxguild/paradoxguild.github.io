Please respect the following rules to ensure a quick and smooth raid:

  • If at any point "clear comms" is called, please respect the call and clear the comms immediately (not when you finish your thought).
  • When we are initiating a boss, DO NOT DPS until it is called for.
    If you wipe the raid because tanks have not positioned or gained enough threat, there will be a penalty.
    Likely leading with a warning, but repeated offenses will be addressed.
  • As we are getting into more complicated raids, it should be expected that you come with your primary consumes, some bandages/healing potions, and at least head and heart world buffs.
    **Please ask if you need help with consumes. If you are unsure about which consumes to bring, ask in your class channels.
  • If you sign up for a raid, or are automatically signed up for a raid (these will be posted every Wednesday), you need to mark if you will be absent or give us head's up.
    Do not sign up & no-show, you will incur a DKP penalty.
    Signups with the "Late" indicator are okay - life happens.
    Just make sure you attempt to show up. If we fill, we will let you know.

Bidding

  • All items start at 5 DKP
  • Bids should be in increments of 5 DKP

Awarding

We award DKP for the following situations:

  • 2 DKP per world buff during Naxx unboon
  • On-Time to raids: 5
  • MC: Bosses add up to 50 DKP
  • BWL: Bosses add up to 50 DKP
  • AQ40: Bosses add up to 100 DKP
  • Naxx: Bosses add up to 250 DKP
  • ZG/AQ20: 20 DKP (these are ran as SR)
  • Onyxia: 15 DKP (this is ran as SR)
  • Donating consumes: up to 30 DKP/wk
  • Prog bonus: 50 DKP (while content is not cleared)
  • Sign up & No-Show: -25 DKP increasing each occurrence - simply communicate in #attendance

Transfers

You can transfer a maximum of 1000 DKP between your toons after a 30% fee (~1430 DKP). You are allowed to do this every 6 weeks per toon.


You are allowed to come on whatever toon that you would like, but if a role is missing or heals/tanks are needed, you may need to hop to that toon as needed. This means you need to save your lockouts if you intend to role swap.


In these cases, you can transfer the DKP earned for that raid (minus whatever you spent) to the toon that you wanted to initially run with, with no fee.


If we need healers to accomplish a raid, you may be asked to change to your healer for that raid.
